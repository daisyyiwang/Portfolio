About Work Resume
I believe good design emerges from “well-formed problems”. It’s essentially an ongoing conversation between designer, people and the community. We inspire each other. 💡
Hi there!👋
I’m Daisy Wang, a product designer based in the bay area with 9+ years of experience. I design both consumer facing and enterprise facing products across devices from desktop, web to mobile.
Currently, my focus is to help integrate creative tools to everyday life at Adobe Document Cloud. My recent work includes unlock MPIP protection within Acrobat desktop, which is nominated as “Security ISV of the year” by Microsoft, also introduced the new Adobe Acrobat Sign add-in for Microsoft Teams.
Prior to that, I was deeply involved in many aspects of the retail world for both online and in-store experience, from online shopping and ship to store, self-checkout at a department store, inventory counting, rewards program to mapping associates experience for product road mapping.
