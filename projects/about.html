YI WANG
Nicknamed Daisy

Daisy is a product designer, an ex-journalist and a passionate drawer.

Drawing makes her to push every pixel. Past journalist experience gives her an eye for good stories. Now as a product designer, she listens to stakeholders and speaks for users.

She believes good design emerges from “well-formed problems”. It should be asthetic, humanistic and systematic. She decided to work as a product designer 5 years ago because of the belief that well-designed techonology could empower more people to get equal access to information and therefore to create a better world.

Her recent focus is about how to build a design system across different channels and how to improve the checkout experience in the store. If you are thinking about the same, reach out!
