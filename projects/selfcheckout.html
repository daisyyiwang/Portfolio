Core Experience Flow

To understand Kohl's customers' expectations and concerns about self-check, when we first started this project, we sent out a national-wide survey. Together with a competitive analysis, we learned that two main concerns Kohl's customers have about self-checkout are:

1. 72% of customers had difficult finding an associate for help.
2. 56% needed associate assistance due to difficulty with applying coupons.

A Closer Look at Design

  • Logic Design for Experience Flow

A. When & how to sign in as a rewards member?

Design Explorations

Final Design

While checking out, theoratically, there are 3 ways for customers to sign in and identify themselves as a rewards member. So initially all the design concepts are displaying these 3 sign-in methods on a side panel of the checkout screen. However, out lab test results show that majority of our users are used to sign in with phone number and they will overlook this sign-in information, which end up with checking out without maximize their offers as a rewards member. To avoid this frustration, the redesigned flow ensured that a modal with a clear sign-in message will display after customers scan all their items and decide to pay.

B. How to design the order confirmation flow within constraints?

Design Explorations

Final Design

In order to push this self-checkout prior to black friday, we will have to design the experience without a paper receipt people usually get in their shopping experience. So how to design the ending part of this experience become a very instesting challenge. This is a seemly simple but rather complicated flow to work with. There are at least 8 different scenarios for the order confirmation flow. It depends on whether or not a purchase has:

1. earned Kohl's Cash?
2. made by a Rewards member?
3. security tags?

Plus, whether or not a customer would like to keep an e-receipt? To design a more intuitive experience, we selected some of the most complicated scenario to test with our customers. After rounds of design iterations and close discussions with stakeholders, the second last design achived an 100% success rate and a 76% overall success rate during usability test.

Associate Support Anytime

The help and associate login button are always placed in the same position so that users can seek support anytime and associate can tap on login without looking at customer's screen.

While offers are preselected, all the Kohl's cash will not be preselected so customers can control when and how much Kohl's cash they would like to apply.
